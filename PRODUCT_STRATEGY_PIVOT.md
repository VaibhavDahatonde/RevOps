## 🎯 The Pivot

A Clari competitor with superior AI forecasting + natural language intelligence layer

---

## 🔥 Why This Pivot? (Customer Discovery)

### What Customers Actually Need:

1. **Better Forecasting Than Salesforce Einstein**
   - Einstein is weak, still needs data analysts
   - Opinion-based stage forecasting (not signal-based)
   - 60-70% accuracy vs our target of 85%+

2. **Instant Answers, Not More Dashboards**
   - "Why did enterprise deals slow down?" → Answer in 20 seconds
   - Tired of spending 20 hours/week on reports nobody reads
   - Need intelligence, not more data visualization

3. **Unified Intelligence Across Fragmented Stack**
   - Data silos: Salesforce + HubSpot + Gong + Slack don't talk
   - Need insights that span all tools
   - Current tools = lots of data, zero synthesis

4. **Real-Time Deal Risk (Not Lagging Indicators)**
   - By the time they see problems in dashboards, deals already lost
   - Need proactive alerts based on behavior, not rep updates
   - Risk scoring that actually predicts outcomes

## 🚀 Go-to-Market Strategy

### Target Customer Profile (ICP):

**Company:**
- Mid-market B2B SaaS ($10M-$100M ARR)
- 50-500 employees
- Using Salesforce + 3-5 other GTM tools
- Growing fast (30%+ YoY)

**Buyer Personas:**

1. **Primary: VP of Revenue Operations**
   - Pain: Drowning in data requests from leadership
   - Goal: Faster insights, better forecasts
   - Budget: $50K-$150K/year for rev ops tools

2. **Secondary: CRO / VP Sales**
   - Pain: Can't trust Salesforce forecasts
   - Goal: Hit quarterly targets, no surprises
   - Influence: Approves RevOps tool spend

3. **End User: Sales Leaders / AEs**
   - Pain: Too much data entry, not enough selling
   - Goal: Know which deals to prioritize
   - Adoption: Use if it saves time

### Distribution Channels:

1. **Salesforce AppExchange** (Primary)
   - Free listing → discovery engine
   - Chrome extension → instant trial
   - Target: 10K installs in 6 months

2. **Slack App Directory** (Secondary)
   - "@RevOpsAI analyze pipeline" → instant value
   - Viral within teams
   - Target: 5K workspace installs

3. **PLG Free Tier** (Growth Engine)
   - Free up to 5 users
   - Limited to 100 queries/month
   - Convert at 15-20% to paid

4. **Content Marketing**
   - SEO: "AI for RevOps", "Clari alternative", "Salesforce forecasting accuracy"
   - Guides: "How to improve forecast accuracy with AI"
   - Case studies: "Company X increased forecast accuracy by 20%"

### Pricing Strategy:

| Tier | Price | Features | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | 100 queries/mo, 5 users, basic integrations | Individual contributors, trial |
| **Pro** | $49/user/mo | Unlimited queries, all integrations, deal risk scoring | Small teams (10-30) |
| **Enterprise** | $99/user/mo | Custom AI agents, white-label, SSO, dedicated support | Large teams (50+) |

**Revenue Model:**
- Month 1-3: Free tier growth (target: 1,000 users)
- Month 4-6: Convert 15% to Pro ($7.3K MRR from 150 users)
- Month 7-12: Scale to 500 paying users ($24.5K MRR)
- Year 2: 2,000 users × $49 = $98K MRR / $1.2M ARR
