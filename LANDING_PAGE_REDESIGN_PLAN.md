## 🚨 Legal Considerations

**Can we say "Clari alternative"?**
✅ Yes, as long as it's factual comparison

**Can we use their name in meta tags?**
✅ Yes, "vs Clari" is fair use (comparative advertising)

**Can we claim "85% accuracy"?**
⚠️ Only if we can prove it (backtest on real data, document methodology)

**Can we show their pricing?**
✅ Yes, if publicly available (cite source: their website, G2 reviews)

**What we CAN'T do:**
❌ Use Clari's logo without permission
❌ Make false claims ("Clari customers hate them")
❌ Imply official partnership/endorsement
❌ Copy their exact design/trade dress
