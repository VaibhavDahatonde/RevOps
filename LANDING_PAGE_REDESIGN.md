# 🎨 RevOps AI Landing Page Redesign

## Design Philosophy

**Inspiration from best-in-class B2B SaaS:**
- Linear: Clean, minimal, purpose-built messaging
- Attio: Colorful, personality-driven, modern
- Stripe: Professional, trust-focused
- Vercel: Product-first, gradient-heavy

**Our Unique Angle:**
- RevOps AI is **AI-native** (not AI-washed)
- Real AI agents that **actually work**
- Built for **RevOps leaders**, not generic sales teams
- Focus on **time savings** and **revenue impact**

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
**Headline:** "Your Revenue Operations Team. Powered by AI."  
**Subhead:** "AI agents that clean your data, predict your forecast, and accelerate deals. While you sleep."  
**CTA:** "See it in action" + "Start free trial"  
**Visual:** Animated product demo or agent dashboard

**Customer Logos:** (if available) or metrics:
- "10K+ deals analyzed"
- "500+ hours saved per team"
- "15% average forecast accuracy improvement"

**Headline:** "Revenue teams waste 40% of their time on manual work"
**3-column pain points:**
- Cleaning duplicate data
- Building forecast models

### 4. Solution Overview
**Headline:** "Meet your AI RevOps team"
**3 AI Agents with cards:**
- Data Hygiene Agent
- Forecast Agent  
- Deal Acceleration Agent

**4-step process:**
1. Connect your CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce)

4. Agents work 24/7 improving your pipeline

### 7. Use Cases
**By Role:**
- RevOps Leaders
- Sales Operations
- Revenue Leaders

### 8. Trust & Security
- SOC 2 Type II (coming soon)
- GDPR compliant
- Enterprise-grade security
- Customer testimonials

### 9. Pricing CTA
**Simple, transparent:** "Start free. Scale when ready."

### 10. Final CTA
**Big, bold:** "Join revenue teams building the future"

## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary:** Purple gradient (#A855F7 → #EC4899)
- **Background:** Ultra-dark (#0A0A0A) with subtle grain texture
- **Cards:** Glass morphism with #18181B + blur
- **Accents:** Cyan for metrics, Green for success

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter/Geist, 72px, Bold, tight leading
- **Body:** Inter, 18px, Regular, relaxed leading
- **Code/Numbers:** JetBrains Mono for metrics

### Components
- Bento grid for features
- Floating cards with shadows
- Subtle animations (not overwhelming)
- Gradient borders on hover

---

## Key Differentiators from Generic Templates

1. **Specific metrics** (not "10X your revenue")
2. **Actual product screenshots** (not stock images)
3. **AI agents have personality** (names, capabilities)
4. **RevOps-specific language** (not generic sales speak)
5. **Technical depth** (show how it works)
6. **Real use cases** (not vague benefits)

---

## Animations

- **Hero:** Typewriter effect for headline
- **Agent cards:** Hover reveals capabilities
- **Stats:** Count-up animation on scroll
- **Product demo:** Auto-playing screencast
- **CTA buttons:** Magnetic cursor effect

---

## Copy Principles

- **Concrete, not abstract:** "Save 10 hours/week" not "Be more productive"
- **RevOps jargon:** Use terms they know (pipeline hygiene, forecast accuracy, deal velocity)
- **Data-driven:** Show real numbers and benchmarks
- **Action-oriented:** Every section has a clear CTA
- **No hype:** Professional tone, not startup-y

---

## Mobile-First

- Single column on mobile
- Simplified animations
- Touch-friendly buttons (44px min)
- Sticky CTA bar on scroll
- Collapsible sections for long content

---

## Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (already using)
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **3D Effects:** Three.js for hero background
- **Gradients:** CSS with grain texture overlay
- **Performance:** Image optimization, lazy loading
